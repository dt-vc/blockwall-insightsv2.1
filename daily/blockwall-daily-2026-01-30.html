This bill grants the Commodity Futures Trading Commission primary regulatory authority over digital commodities and spot markets while establishing consumer protections. Chairman John Boozman led the effort after bipartisan talks with Senator Cory Booker failed due to policy disagreements and ethics concerns over public officials in crypto.
