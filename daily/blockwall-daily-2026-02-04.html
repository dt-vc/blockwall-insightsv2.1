Crypto crime-fighting startup TRM Labs notches $1 billion valuation with new $70 million funding round

Leo Schwartz • Security • Score: 7/10

TRM Labs, a leading provider of blockchain analytics tools for detecting and investigating crypto-related crimes, raised $70 million in funding to reach a $1 billion valuation. The platform enables financial institutions, law enforcement agencies like the FBI, and crypto businesses to trace illicit funds across blockchains, identify real-world entities behind addresses, and generate court-ready evidence. This milestone underscores growing institutional demand for reliable blockchain intelligence amid rising crypto fraud, ransomware, and sanctions evasion risks. For institutional investors, TRM's expansion signals a maturing compliance infrastructure essential for scaling crypto asset management while meeting AML/CFT standards.
