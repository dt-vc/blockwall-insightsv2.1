Crypto firms raise $258m despite $2 trillion market drawdown

Lance Datskoluo • Investment • Score: 7/10

Crypto firms raised $258 million in funding this week, including Tether's $100 million investment in Anchorage Digital, TRM Labs' $70 million Series C round, and Jupiter's $35 million from ParaFi Capital, despite a $2 trillion cryptocurrency market drawdown. These investments matter as they bolster regulated services essential for broader adoption by asset managers and corporations.
